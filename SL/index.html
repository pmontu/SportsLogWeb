Gym Log: do you visit the gym and record your weights and repetitions. Have you always thought of something better than paper and pen. Have you always wanted to try recording your stastistics. Did you want a very simple tool to do it for you.

Here is the solution to all your gym log problems. Even access the website via mobile and record statistics as you workout. Signup and login and you will be guided by a tutorial till you get used to the simple user interface.





Movie Lists: are you a movie buff, who records the movies you have watched, you have come to the right place, record your movies here and share with your friends.
Login Signup
Login: type in your user id and password if you allready have an account or signup with us to use our services.

Signup: to signup, simply enter a new user id and password into the fields above and click on signup. You would then be able to log into the application.

Tip: the plus signs appear at 4 places, clicking on them will open a textbox and a save button. Typing a text and saving will save the data.

Info: this site has been developed with webservice backends running on php mysql linux servers on bluehost.com. The html and javascript comprise of the client side code which peform ajax calls to send and retrieve data to the server.
